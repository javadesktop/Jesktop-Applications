HorstScape2

Like Horstscape, Horstscape2 uses a jar file from Horst's website. This time it is webwindow.jar and Horst has given permission for it to be distributed with Jesktop. Apart from being a general update, this component is a self limit demo of Horst's commercial component. After 15 mins it displays a dialog warning of its demo status and then disables itself. Horst has givn permission for us to distribute the webwindow jar file with this forked version of his browser.

License

Horst's Browser source is free for reuse, but the rendering component is commercial for anything other than a 15 minute demo.
